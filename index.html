Trezor.io Start® - Starting Up Your Device* | Trezor.io

Welcome to your guide for setting up the Trezor Wallet version 22.9.3! If you're new to Trezor or need a refresher, follow these simple steps to get started and secure your digital assets.

Step 1: Visit the Trezor Start Page

Begin by navigating to the Trezor Start page. This page is your gateway to setting up your Trezor wallet and ensures you have the latest version, 22.9.3, installed for optimal security and functionality.

Step 2: Connect Your Trezor Device

Once you’re on the Trezor Start page, connect your Trezor device to your computer using a USB cable. Your device will automatically be detected, and you’ll see a prompt to proceed with the setup.

Step 3: Install the Trezor Bridge or Trezor Suite

To manage your wallet, you need the Trezor Bridge or Trezor Suite. If you haven't installed them yet, follow the instructions provided on the Trezor Start page. For detailed installation guides, you can refer to the Trezor documentation. This ensures you're using the latest software compatible with version 22.9.3.

Step 4: Set Up Your Wallet

After installing the necessary software, you will be guided through the process of setting up your wallet. This involves creating a PIN for secure access and writing down your recovery seed phrase. The seed phrase is crucial as it allows you to recover your wallet in case of loss or damage.

Step 5: Configure Security Features

Enhance your wallet's security by configuring additional features. This includes setting up two-factor authentication and exploring other security options available in the Trezor Suite.

Step 6: Verify and Backup

Verify that all your settings are correct and back up your recovery seed in a safe place. Your recovery seed is a critical component of your wallet's security, so handle it with care.

Additional Resources

For more detailed instructions and troubleshooting, check out the Trezor Start Documentation. For further assistance, visit the Trezor Support Page for expert help.

By following these steps, you'll have your Trezor Wallet version 22.9.3 up and running in no time, ensuring your digital assets are secure and easily accessible.
